How It Works

Three steps to real-time clarity.

Sales Pulse Live reads your order and transaction data and exports it in structured formats so you can analyze your shop performance anywhere.

01
🔐

Connect Your Shop

Authenticate securely using Etsy's official OAuth 2.0 login. We never see or store your password.

02
⚡

Sync Your Data

Sales Pulse Live reads your sales, orders, and receipt data in real time directly from the Etsy Open API v3.

03
📤

Export & Analyze

Download your data as CSV or JSON and plug it into any spreadsheet, dashboard, or analytics tool you prefer.
