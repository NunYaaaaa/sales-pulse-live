How It Works

Three steps to real-time clarity.

Sales Pulse Live reads your order and transaction data and exports it in structured formats so you can analyze your shop anywhere.

01
🔐

Connect Your Shop

Authenticate securely using Etsy's official OAuth 2.0 login. We never see or store your password.

02
⚡

Sales Pulse Live reads your sales, orders, and receipt data in real time directly from the Etsy Open API v3.

Download as CSV or JSON and plug into any spreadsheet, dashboard, or analytics tool you prefer.
